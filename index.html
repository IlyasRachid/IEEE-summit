IEEE Morocco Blockchain Summit

INPT, and the IEEE Morocco Section, are delighted to announce the organization of the second edition of the IEEE Morocco Blockchain Summit. The event will take place, in hybride mode, on February 22, 2024, in INPT, Rabat, Morocco. Blockchain technology has emerged as a revolutionary approach towards strengthening digital trust, but many challenges and open issues persist, potentially inhibiting the widespread adoption of Blockchain in critical organizations and ecosystems. This event is an opportunity for different actors and stakeholders, from academia, industry, and various organizations, to exchange and discuss research results, case studies, opportunities, challenges, and emerging directions related to Blockchain technology.

Day 1: Capture The Flag (CTF)

Sunday, February 15, 2025

The "SecDojo" team will host the Capture The Flag competition, providing participants with a platform to showcase their technical expertise in Blockchain security and related fields. This initiative promotes innovation, collaboration, and hands-on learning in a competitive yet educational environment.
